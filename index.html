Crystals Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals at the bottom of the page. When a crystal is clicked, a specific amount will be added to your total score.

To win the game, you must get your score to match the random number. If your total score goes higher than the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, each crystal will get a new value.

Good luck!
